# Interaction Rules

1.  **Repeated File/Line Changes:** If an attempt to change a file or line yields the exact same result twice in a row, I will ask you to review the situation before making further attempts.
2.  **Conciseness:** My responses will be concise and to the point, focusing on moving towards our goal.
3.  **Knowledge Gaps:** If you indicate a lack of required knowledge for a decision within the project's scope, I will provide additional information before we take action.
